The crashed spacestation under Berlin designed and build a miniature model (Scale 1:42) of the mainantenna of our spacestation for CCCamp. It was 9.40m high and the big ball was rotating.

Construction of Tower

First, we built the base of the Tower in the c-base mainhall

The Ball

The Ball is made of a lightweight wooden construction with polystyrene ribs around it. It was later covered with several layers of paper, aluminiumfoil and waterproof paint

Test Erection

We tested the procedure of erection in c-base, before we took it all apart and shipped it to camp. We learned that erecting it is easy with 6-10 strong people and erection does not need further tools apart from strong volunteers.
